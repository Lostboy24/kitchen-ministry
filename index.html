The Mandate

Kitchen Ministry was founded on one belief: vegetarian food deserves the full ceremony — fire, spice, and a menu written like a proper decree.

Set in Udasar, Bikaner, the kitchen runs a purely vegetarian charter across Asian fusion, Chinese and continental plates — from smoking hot Manchow to a Golden Thali built for the table. No compromises, no meat, and no shortage of flavour.

Evenings unfold by the fireplace. Regulars know to arrive hungry, stay for happy hour, and leave having eaten more than they planned.
